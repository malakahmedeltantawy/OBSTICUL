How to Play

  • SPACEBAR: Jump (Press 2x in the air to Double Jump over tall Red blocks!).
  • LEFT / RIGHT Arrows (or A/D): Move sideways to slide through Orange wall gaps.
  • DOWN Arrow (or S): Duck on the ground to slide under Purple ceiling bars (Hard Mode).
  • Collect Gold Coins for a bonus +50 points!
